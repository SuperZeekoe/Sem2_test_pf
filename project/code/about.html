About Erion

He graduated from the Athens School of Fine Arts in 2013, specializing in painting and sculpture. His works, whether paintings or sculptures, are small problems that he sets for himself and seeks to solve within the framework of the visual language. Due to his strong need to approach every kind of creative challenge, Erion Kapaj devotes a lot of time to designing and constructing various creations that combine mathematics and engineering. For him, these constructions are simply games that allow him to pass his time in a pleasant and creative way.
