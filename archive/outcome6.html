Professional Identity

A professional identity is definitely something I feel like I’m forming for myself. I consistently have a ton of crazy ideas I might want to implement or create a website around. Coming from Semester 1 where I may have over-asked myself and had to compromise on a lot in the end, I intend on taking things a bit slower for semester 2. I want to consistently improve bit upon bit on this portfolio.

The skeleton frame of the website is there but there are a ton of features I would like to implement over the course of the semester. Including a sideways scroll function, possible one-page solution for all content and more.

With the JavaScript classes we’re getting currently I think a lot of these things could become reality in the near future, with a bit of creative googling to find others’ ideas and thoughts on possible solutions for my self-made problems.

A big addition to my identity as a designer is coming from my logo. Previously I didn’t have anything regarding a logo but now the Manatee is coming to life. Of course it would require some extra iterations to become perfect but for now I am extremely pleased with myself.

I feel myself becoming creative just studying media design and I want to keep this going. I write down my ideas more often and keep them in my pocket for when I actually want to add them to future or current projects. The best change for myself so far has been the feedback I received from Josh, and I paraphrase: Rather do something simple, perfectly, than doing something outrageous, sloppy.
