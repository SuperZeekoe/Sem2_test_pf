UCD Assignments

A few assignments we received for UCD were to create some short presentations on different subjects.

The first presentation was focussed around our own personal views on our future regarding UCD, as well as finding some examples of good and bad website designs. I feel like my future in UCD lies mainly within continuous innovation and imagination. As long as I can keep improving myself and staying creative I feel like everything will fall into place. I tend to have big ideas initially but I need to get myself to a point where I can fully execute those crazy ideas.

The examples I found for good designs were mostly focussed on those crazy or innovating ideas too. Some looking more like game menus or interactive experiences, rather than standard websites. The bad websites had fairly poor UX in common. On those it was hard to find specifically what you’d want, hence me putting them on the ‘bad’ list.

The second being a short Coca Cola assignment. Gathering minor data on the company and how they partake in market segmentation. This being a group assignment we all found the same data initially. I mainly focussed on the design of the presentation while others gathered most of the data.

The third presentation was based on different psychological philosophies. We were tasked to find examples for each of them. I feel like I successfully completed this assignment, finding good examples for each principle. Some of the examples from the presentation I made are: Path of leasts Resistance (Netflix), Perpetual Habit (Youtube), and Magical Number Seven (Coolblue).
