Logo Design

My logo design story started off with a bunch of ideas coming from either my gamertag or my last name. Initially I wanted to draw a very minimalistic looking manatee. I feel like I succeeded, but when I asked people to identify what my logo looked like, they couldn’t see anything resembling an animal, let alone a manatee.

Because of that I started looking at other options of designs, going to my last name of ‘Lemmens’. This is pronounced as Lemons in English so I thought it’d be funny if I maybe created a lemon tree out of an ‘L’. This idea didn’t really go anywhere. I tried creating a few lemons to go along with my name but nothing looked right, so I shelved this idea. I went back to the manatee drawing board.

A tip from Robertas was to lower my mouse speed as low as it can go, and draw lines that way. So I found a manatee image to draw the outlines from for the initial form. After that I tweaked it a bit with some accents and shadows and there we go, a clean manatee. I’m really happy with the result as it kept my main idea of keeping a minimalist approach.

Next up was the typography. I went for SZC, combining my gamertag of ‘Super Zeekoe’ and ‘Coding’. After a couple of iterations I found a font I liked, now all that was left was colours. In my initial portfolio designs I had a few colours as options for accents, one being yellow and another option was red. After using them individually for the logo, I tried combining them and I instantly fell in love. It did however remind of a faction in a videogame though, but coincidentally that was my favourite faction in said game. I toyed a bunch with drop-shadows and ended up on only using a drop-shadow on the C, it felt a bit much if it was on everything. The last accent I added was a slight red outer-glow on the C and the Manatee to make those two pop a bit more and voila, SZC.
