Communication

This learning outcome has been one of the more consistent ones for me. I value good communication and try my best to keep communication between everything clear. For the project that means coming up with short day plans, to give everyone an idea of the things we have to do. I was also the contact person between our project client and our group, scheduling the meeting for an interview.

With fellow classmates I tend to give and take feedback quite often. Mostly sharing ideas with my project group, showing different variations on designs, sharing thoughts on the UI/UX of a portfolio or general design aspects. For the programming classes I’ve talked a lot to Rob in case I wasn’t sure about something JavaScript related. I also asked Frank for some feedback when I got stuck on an assignment, which then greatly helped me along and got me to the point where I could complete the assignment.

One big improvement I have is to more actively seek our teacher feedback. Looking back I feel like I’ve been slightly neglecting it unintentionally. Mostly because I feel like I was doing fine regardless, but in the future I should reach out more. Even if I’m still fine, a bit of feedback never hurts, as there is always something to improve upon. I have reached out a few times, regarding some coding and some logo design aspects, but I should ask for more feedback and help regarding the project and research as I’m less confident in those aspects.
