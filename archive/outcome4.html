Research

We’re currently still in the researching phase for our project. Together with Luc, we’re currently working on a competitor analysis. We are looking at a variety of different artists websites to find inspiration, advantage and definite no-go’s. Our current finding is that our client has a unique position with his clock art. Besides that his use of different shades of white can really set him apart from other artists out there currently.

I’ve also worked together with my group on defining our main research question as well as sub-questions. Trying to come up with well linked questions that can answer our main question. One question I came up with for example: How can Erion’s art best be used as a basis for a website.

This links into our main question “What would a showcase of Erion’s portfolio and personality look like, and how can that be incorporated into a website?”. If we can answer that sub-question we can answer a large part of our main question going forward. Creating a design for the website.

I also took part in preparation of and the actual interview with our client. For the preparation part I was the contact person between our client and our group. I scheduled the meeting and for the interview I was there taking notes and starting the recording after it was agreed upon. I tried to keep the notes as clear as possible for the rest of the group to use. During the interview we noticed the client drastically changed his approach from the initial meeting at the start of the semester. Mainly in his take on a shop function for the website. This is crucial information we’re glad to have received through the interview, because without one we wouldn’t have known this was now one of his wants.
